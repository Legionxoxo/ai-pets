# AI Pets (Trickcal Chibi Go Pet)

<img src="logo.png" width="150" align="right" alt="AI Pets Logo">

Bring adorable, interactive **Trickcal Revive** chibi characters to life on your favorite chatbot websites! 

These cute pets will wander around your screen, stand on your chat input box, and keep you company while you work or chat.

## ðŸ“¦ Installation

1.  **Download** the extension code (clone this repo).
2.  Open your browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
3.  Navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
4.  Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in the top-right corner).
5.  Click **Load unpacked**.
6.  Select the `extension` folder from this project.
7.  Go to any supported chatbot site and say hi to your new friends!
